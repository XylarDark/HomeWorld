# HomeWorld – Prototype Vision (One-Pager)

**Theme:** "Love as Epic Quest" – Dopamine from intense combat + Oxytocin from nurturing bonds. Players build and protect a life worth fighting for. Roles for casual (healer/home) and hardcore (protector) players.

**Full campaign & moral vision:** For the complete narrative (homestead tutorial → family taken → 7 sin-themed levels → true villain → ruined homestead day/night loop → final boss & succession), death/succession rules, and the Seven Sins vs Virtues moral compass (-1/0/+1, rewards, endgame), see [CAMPAIGN_VISION.md](CAMPAIGN_VISION.md).

**Act 1 focus (Lone Wanderer):** Crash-land, scout biomes, fight bosses, claim home. Solo, 2–3h. Emotional beat: isolation → determination.

**Week 1 playtest goal:** Survive 3 missions: crash → scout → boss → claim home.

**Tech spine:** PCG forest biome, GAS (3 skills), proc-gen realms, basic building. Free assets: FAB survival char, Quixel biomes.

**Success criteria:** First playable loop = explore → fight → build. No family or co-op yet.
